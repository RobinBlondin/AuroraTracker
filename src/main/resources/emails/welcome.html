Welcome to Aurora Tracker, and thank you for joining our community of skywatchers.

You will now receive notifications when the northern lights are likely to be visible near your location under favorable weather conditions. Our system analyzes geomagnetic activity, cloud coverage, and regional visibility factors to detect the best possible moments for aurora observation.
